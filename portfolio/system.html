Custom Websites

I build professional, mobile-responsive websites tailored specifically to your business goals and brand identity. Unlike template-based sites, I create custom-coded solutions that give you complete control over design, functionality, and user experience. Each website is built with clean, semantic HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, ensuring fast loading speeds even on Kenya's mobile networks. I focus on creating intuitive navigation, clear calls-to-action, and SEO-friendly structures that help your business get found on Google. Every site includes a content management system (CMS) that lets you update text, images, and products without needing technical skills. Whether you need a corporate brochure site, a service-based landing page, or a multi-page business website, I deliver a solution that represents your brand professionally and converts visitors into customers.

Business Systems

I develop custom management systems that automate and streamline your day-to-day operations, eliminating manual paperwork and spreadsheets. These are web-based dashboards built specifically for Kenyan businesses, accessible from any device with an internet connection. Depending on your needs, I create systems for tenant and property management, school administration (student records, fees tracking, exam management), church management (membership, contributions, events), inventory control, or HR and payroll. Each system features role-based access so staff only see what they need, real-time reporting dashboards with exportable data, automated SMS/email notifications, and secure data backup. I integrate M-Pesa APIs for automated payment tracking, reconciliation, and receipt generation. The goal is to give you a centralized platform that saves hours of manual work, reduces errors, and gives you instant visibility into your business performance.
