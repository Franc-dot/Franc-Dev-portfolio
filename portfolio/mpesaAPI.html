M-Pesa API Integration

I integrate Safaricom's M-Pesa API into your business systems, enabling seamless mobile money transactions directly from your website or application. This includes STK Push (Lipa Na M-Pesa Online) for instant payment prompts on customer phones, C2B (Customer to Business) for receiving payments, B2C (Business to Customer) for sending payouts or refunds, and transaction validation with automatic receipt generation. All integrations are secure, PCI-compliant, and designed to work with Kenya's internet reliability — including fallback mechanisms for network fluctuations. Whether you're running an e-commerce store, rental business, or service platform, I ensure your customers can pay using M-Pesa without leaving your site.
